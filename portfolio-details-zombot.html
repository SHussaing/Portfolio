Zombot - Unity Game Project

Zombot is a Unity-based survival game developed as part of my Games Project course at Bahrain Polytechnic. In this action-packed game, players face waves of zombies across four increasingly challenging stages. The objective is to survive each wave by utilizing a combination of strategic movement, weapon upgrades, and resource management.

As players progress, they earn points for every zombie defeated, which can be redeemed in the in-game shop to upgrade weapons like the knife and grenade. These upgrades are essential for handling the tougher waves in later stages, adding a strategic layer to the gameplay.

Zombot showcases my skills in Unity game development, including 3D design, enemy AI, and game mechanics. I designed the gameplay to be engaging and rewarding, with a balanced difficulty progression that keeps players motivated to improve and reach higher stages.
